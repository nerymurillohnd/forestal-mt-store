## Executive Summary

Forestal MT is a Honduran, family-origin export company positioned in the premium segment, specializing in ethnobotanical products and natural raw materials. The company serves international buyers, brands, distributors, and manufacturers through a dual model: **B2B wholesale/private label** and **direct-to-consumer (B2C)**. Forestal MT focuses on long-term commercial relationships, consistent supply, and professional export execution.

**Slogan:** Exporting Nature Without Borders

## Sourcing Model, Social Responsibility, and Product Disclaimer

### Sourcing Model

Forestal MT does not manufacture or fabricate products. All products are sourced directly from native communities and indigenous peoples of Honduras through long-term alliances built on mutual trust and shared value.

| Field              | Value                                                              |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Production model   | Artisanal, community-sourced                                       |
| Manufacturing      | None — Forestal MT is a sourcing, export, and distribution company |
| Supply partners    | Native communities and indigenous peoples of Honduras              |
| Relationship model | Long-term alliances and direct partnerships                        |
| Processing methods | Traditional, ancestral techniques passed through generations       |

### Social Responsibility

| Principle              | Description                                                                  |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fair compensation      | Equitable and fair payments to all sourcing communities                      |
| Cultural respect       | Respect for traditions, cultural practices, and ancestral knowledge          |
| Heritage preservation  | Preservation of indigenous techniques and ethnobotanical heritage            |
| Ethical supply chain   | Transparent and traceable chain from origin community to export              |
| Sustainable harvesting | Practices that protect ecosystems and ensure long-term resource availability |

### Product Disclaimer

**Traditional use products.** Forestal MT products are rooted in traditional ethnobotanical knowledge of indigenous peoples and native communities of Honduras. They are intended for traditional use only.

**Not medical products.** Products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, heal, or prevent any disease or medical condition. They do not replace professional medical treatment, advice, or consultation. Product descriptions that reference traditional uses (supports, promotes, traditional applications) reflect ancestral indigenous herbalism practices and cultural context — they are not medical or therapeutic claims.

**Consult a specialist.** Customers should consult a qualified healthcare professional before using any product, especially if pregnant, nursing, taking medication, or managing a medical condition.

**Age restriction.** Products are recommended for persons aged 13 and above. Not recommended for children under 12 years of age.

**Artisanal nature.** All products are artisanal and processed using traditional methods by sourcing communities. Natural variations in color, texture, aroma, and appearance between batches are normal and reflect the authentic, unrefined nature of the products.

**FDA disclaimer.** These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. Products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
